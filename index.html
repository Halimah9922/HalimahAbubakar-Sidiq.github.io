ABOUT ME

Hi, My name is Halimah and I work remotely as a freelance Data Analyst.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Systems Engineering from the University of Lagos. I specialize in translating complex data into valuable insights using my skills in Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, and Tableau. With expertise in both machine learning and data visualization, I apply supervised and unsupervised algorithms to uncover patterns and trends that drive informed decision-making. My approach to data analysis is like solving a puzzle—finding hidden patterns and insights is both a challenge and a passion for me.

In addition to my technical skills, I am actively involved in the data science community through volunteer work with Statistics Without Borders. I also share my insights and experiences on my data blog on Medium, where I explore various aspects of data science and analytics.

If you’re interested in collaborating or want to learn more about my work, feel free to reach out to me via email or LinkedIn.
